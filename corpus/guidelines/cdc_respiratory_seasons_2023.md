# CDC Guideline: Respiratory_Seasons (2023)

## Overview
CDC guidance for respiratory seasons.

## Principles
Adapt to local policy. Perform risk assessment. Non-prescriptive baseline.

## Recommendations

- promote vaccination and masking.
- issue public health advisories.

## Implementation
Train staff; monitor adherence; document deviations; quarterly review.

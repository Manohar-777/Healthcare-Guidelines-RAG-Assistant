# WHO Guideline: Ppe (2019)

## Overview
WHO guidance for ppe.

## Principles
Adapt to local policy. Perform risk assessment. Non-prescriptive baseline.

## Recommendations
- select PPE based on risk assessment.
- use gloves gowns eye protection when exposure risk exists.
- don and doff safely per protocol.

## Implementation
Train staff; monitor adherence; document deviations; quarterly review.

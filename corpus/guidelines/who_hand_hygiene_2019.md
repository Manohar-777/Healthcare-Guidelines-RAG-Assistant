# WHO Guideline: Hand_Hygiene (2019)

## Overview
WHO guidance for hand hygiene.

## Principles
Adapt to local policy. Perform risk assessment. Non-prescriptive baseline.

## Recommendations
- perform hand hygiene before touching a patient.
- after touching a patient.
- before aseptic procedures.
- after body fluid exposure.
- after touching patient surroundings.

## Implementation
Train staff; monitor adherence; document deviations; quarterly review.

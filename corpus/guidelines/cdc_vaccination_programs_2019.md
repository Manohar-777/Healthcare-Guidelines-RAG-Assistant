# CDC Guideline: Vaccination_Programs (2019)

## Overview
CDC guidance for vaccination programs.

## Principles
Adapt to local policy. Perform risk assessment. Non-prescriptive baseline.

## Recommendations
- prioritize high risk groups.
- ensure cold chain integrity.
- record doses accurately.

## Implementation
Train staff; monitor adherence; document deviations; quarterly review.

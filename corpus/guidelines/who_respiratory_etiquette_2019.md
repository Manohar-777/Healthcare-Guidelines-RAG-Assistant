# WHO Guideline: Respiratory_Etiquette (2019)

## Overview
WHO guidance for respiratory etiquette.

## Principles
Adapt to local policy. Perform risk assessment. Non-prescriptive baseline.

## Recommendations
- encourage mask use in respiratory seasons.
- cover coughs and sneezes.
- maintain distance where feasible.

## Implementation
Train staff; monitor adherence; document deviations; quarterly review.

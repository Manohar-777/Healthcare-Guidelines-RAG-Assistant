# WHO Guideline: Environmental_Cleaning (2022)

## Overview
WHO guidance for environmental cleaning.

## Principles
Adapt to local policy. Perform risk assessment. Non-prescriptive baseline.

## Recommendations
- routine cleaning with approved disinfectants.
- increase frequency for high touch surfaces.
- respect manufacturer contact times.

## Implementation
Train staff; monitor adherence; document deviations; quarterly review.

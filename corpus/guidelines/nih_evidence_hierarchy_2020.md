# NIH Guideline: Evidence_Hierarchy (2020)

## Overview
NIH guidance for evidence hierarchy.

## Principles
Adapt to local policy. Perform risk assessment. Non-prescriptive baseline.

## Recommendations
- systematic reviews and randomized trials provide higher certainty.
- observational studies offer moderate certainty.
- expert opinion has lower certainty.

## Implementation
Train staff; monitor adherence; document deviations; quarterly review.

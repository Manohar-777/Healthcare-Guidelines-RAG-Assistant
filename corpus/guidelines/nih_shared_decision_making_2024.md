# NIH Guideline: Shared_Decision_Making (2024)

## Overview
NIH guidance for shared decision making.

## Principles
Adapt to local policy. Perform risk assessment. Non-prescriptive baseline.

## Recommendations
- clinicians and patients collaborate on decisions.
- consider patient values and preferences.
- present benefits and risks transparently.

## Implementation
Train staff; monitor adherence; document deviations; quarterly review.

# NIH Guideline: Grading (2022)

## Overview
NIH guidance for grading.

## Principles
Adapt to local policy. Perform risk assessment. Non-prescriptive baseline.

## Recommendations
- strong recommendations when benefits outweigh harms.
- conditional recommendations when balance is uncertain.

## Implementation
Train staff; monitor adherence; document deviations; quarterly review.
